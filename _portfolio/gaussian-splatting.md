## Overview
Led development of a pipeline for creating "Digital Twins" of objects through Gaussian Splatting at KEF Robotics. This technique enables high-fidelity 3D reconstruction for autonomous drone navigation and inspection.

## Technical Details
- **Framework**: PyTorch
- **Application**: 3D reconstruction for UAV navigation
- **Organization**: KEF Robotics

## Impact
Enables precise 3D modeling of real-world environments for autonomous drone operations, improving navigation accuracy and inspection capabilities.
